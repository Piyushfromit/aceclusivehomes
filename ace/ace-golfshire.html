ACE Golfshire

ACE Golf Shire is a luxurious residential project located on the Noida Expressway in Noida. The project offers 455 units spread over 5.7 acres. The apartments are luxurious with a range of sizes from 2BHK to 3BHK. The project offers a seamless connectivity to The Grand Venice Mall and Gautam Buddha University. The project also offers a range of amenities such as a clubhouse, swimming pool, gymnasium, children’s play area and landscaped gardens.
ACE Golfshire in Sector 150, Noida is a ready-to-move housing society. It offers apartments in varied budget range. These units are a perfect combination of comfort and style, specifically designed to suit your requirements and conveniences. There are 2BHK, 3BHK and 4BHK Apartments available in this project. This housing society is now ready to be called home as families have started moving in. Check out some of the features of ACE Golfshire housing society:
*ACE Golfshire Sector 150 has 5 towers, with 22 floors each and 455 units on offer.
*Spread over an area of 6.25 acres, ACE Golfshire is one of the spacious housing societies in the Noida region. With all the basic amenities available, ACE Golfshire fits into your budget and your lifestyle.
*Sector 150 has good connectivity to some of the important areas in the proximity such as Arya Hospital, Shaheed Bhagat Singh Park and Greater Valley School and so on.
