ACE Aqua Casa

Ace Aqua Casa Noida Extension offers lavish exteriors and opulent interiors that are simply incomparable. Due to its prime location, it is the first lakeside residential complex globally, offering its residents a tranquil and peaceful environment. Ace Aqua Casa residences are known for their excellent amenities and facilities, which give them a prime attraction among residents.

The Amenities at Ace Aqua Casa

The Ace Aqua Casa is proud to offer a broad range of amenities designed for the residents to enjoy. The premises are equipped with a swimming pool with clean water and are well maintained. This project is extremely positive for gymnasts since it also contains a gymnasium, making it perfect for you. If you have children, you certainly do not need to be concerned about where they will play. You will find a kid's play area in the project that will be great for you and your children.

A couple of highlights from the project

  • The total area of this project is 10,76 acres (43.54 kilometers square meters), with 1478 units and 16 towers, with 21 floors.
  • This is the first lake city in Noida. It is a green retreat surrounded by the city, a 2.5-acre space in the middle of the city.
  • The project consists mainly of a podium-based architecture concept.
  • It has a plot of land that is open on three sides.
  • All buildings are designed according to Vaastu principles and are environmentally friendly.
  • It is surrounded by a 24-hour security complex with a gated entrance.
